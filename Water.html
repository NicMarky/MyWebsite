Mini Water Level Sensor

Core of the system
Water Level Sensor
Final View

This is a Mini Water Level Sensor made of basic logic gates (ic).
Four wires placed at different heights act as probes; when water touches them, it completes a circuit. Basic gates process these signals to light up indicators. The buzzers on the top two levels serve as critical "overflow" or "high-water" alarms to prevent flooding. It simulates a water level sensor which the Led lights serves as the level indicator at each level of the water level.
The last 2 led light will be on together with the buzzer indicating the water level is about to overflow.
