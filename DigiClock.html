This is a Digital Clock which is made of Logic Gates (ic).
A timing circuit generates regular pulse signals that act as the clock source, allowing the digits to count seconds, minutes, and hours sequentially. The counters are cascaded so that when one digit reaches 9, it resets to 0 and triggers the next digit to increment.
